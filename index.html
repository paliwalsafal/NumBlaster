NumBlaster is an one-place destination for all puzzle geeks. You can play here most interesting puzzles of all times i.e, Sudoku and 2048. Sudoku, on one hand,is a logic-based, combinatorial number-placement puzzle with the goal to fill a 9×9 grid with numbers so that each row, column and 3×3 section contain all of the digits between 1 and 9 whereas 2048 is a sliding block puzzle with an aim to combine the numbers displayed on the tiles until you reach 2048.
